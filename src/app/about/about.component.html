ABOUT US

Our Vision: Tiny Homes, Big Impact

At Grannyflats, we believe in the power of tiny homes to create meaningful change. Our mission is to provide innovative, sustainable, and affordable housing solutions that inspire and empower people to live more intentionally.

Grannyflats was founded by a team of passionate builders, designers, and environmental advocates who share a common goal to revolutionize the way we think about living spaces. With years of experience in the construction and design industries, we bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project.
